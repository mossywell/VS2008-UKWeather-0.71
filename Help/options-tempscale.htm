Options - Temperature Scale

Use the check boxes to select either degress Celsius (centigrade) or degrees Farenheit. Note the following when changing the temperature scale.
  1. The Temperature in Farenheit is calculated from the Celsius temperature rather than obtained directly from the web site. Therefore, the temperature in degrees Farenheit may be slightly different (by one degree sometimes) from the temperature displayed on the web site. The technical reason is that the web site actually does a series of redirections (4 at the time of writing) if Farenheit is chosen. The redirections are based on cookies dropped onto the PC during the redirections. UK Weather, however, is not written to parse cookies or indeed the javascript code that does the redirection. Therefore, the simpler option to obtain the raw data from the web site in degress Celsius and simply converting it has been chosen instead.
  2. If the temperature scale is changed, it will not show until the end of the current update interval. Therefore, to see the change immediately, select "OK and Update", or "Update Now" from the menu. (This behaviour may change in future versions.)
